Wǎngluò yòngyǔ 网络用语

Literally 'network language' - means language of the internet. (i.e. 'LOL' in english)

One example is túrán 突然 - literally means suddenly; can also be used in 'internet speak' to call someone ugly

非常 - fei means "not", 不 常 is common, like 常常 - so togethr, it means not common

帮忙 bāng máng

Other words meaning help:

  • 帮助 - bāng zhù
  • 帮忙 - bāng máng； think of this like v. + o.; it's not 1 ci.

Sentences (for bāng zhù):

  • 谢谢你帮（助）我。
  • 谢谢你的帮助。
  • 谢谢你给我的帮助。

Sentences for bāng máng

  • 谢谢你给我帮忙。
  • 请你帮我一个忙。
