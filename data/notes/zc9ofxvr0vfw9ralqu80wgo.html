提供 tí gòng - offer, included with / complimentary

These words must be together to have this meaning.

宾馆提供早餐。Bīn guǎn tí gòng zǎo cān。 - The hotel has complimentary breakfast.

提供工作机会 Tí gòng gōng zuò jī huì - Job offers

It's a verb:

s. + 提供 + obj (something good) 福利 fú lì - welfare /机会/ etc.

尽管 jǐn guǎn - albeit

Similar structure and meaning to 虽然。。。但是, you can say 尽管。。。还是。

  • 虽然汉语很难，但是我想学。
  • 尽管汉语很难，我还是想学。

Using 尽管 puts more emphasis on the extent than 虽然.

The connector word can be

  • 但是
  • 可是
  • 却 still (new word, see below). It should be placed after the subject:
    • 尽管汉语很难，我却很想学。
