Vocabulary

比较 bǐ jiào

Meanings:

  1. compare. Colloquially can say 比一比
  2. to a greater extent than.
    • 我比较喜欢喝拿铁。 (拿铁 - ná tiě, latté)
    • 他比较高。

一定 yiding

meaning - definitely

肯定 kěn ding - even stronger

参加 cān jiā

参 - by itself, it means to want

了解 jiě

  1. to learn
  2. to know someone very well (wo ren shi ta, dan shi wo bu liao jie ta).
  3. to know about a certain thing or situation.
    1. 我不了解他
    2. 我对他不了解
    3. 我们前了解一下情况，再。。。 (情况 qing kuang - situation?)

Levels of acquaintance:

  • 知道
  • 认识
  • 熟悉
  • 了解
  • 理解

解, by itself, means to untie (a knot)
