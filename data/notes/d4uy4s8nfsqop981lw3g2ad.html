Vocabulary

宝贵 bǎoguì - valuable

  • very often used with '宝贵经验' bǎoguì jīngyàn - Valuable experience
  • and also 我的时间很宝贵，我不想浪费。Wǒ deshíjiān hěn bǎoguì,wǒbùxiǎnglàngfèi. - My time is precious and I don't want to waste it.
