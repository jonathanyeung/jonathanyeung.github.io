## Vocabulary

_outside of HSK_

- 主动 zhǔdòng - active
- 格外 géwài - particularly
- 夸张 kuāzhāng - exaggeration
- 极其 jíqí - exceedingly - usage is the same as 特别, but used for more important / formal things.
