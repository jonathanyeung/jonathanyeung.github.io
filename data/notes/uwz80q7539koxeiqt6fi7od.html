Difference between 刚 and 刚才

Duration of time. 刚 is maybe 1 - 5 minutes prior to the current point in time. 刚才 is maybe 5-30 minutes.

  • A: 刚才，我给你发微信，你怎么没回我？
  • B: 刚才，我在上课。我刚下课，刚看到微信。

（微信 wēi xìn - Wechat）

  • 我上个月刚来北京。
  • 我十点刚下课，10:10分不可能到你家。

你为什么刚才不说，现在才说？nǐ wèi shén me gāng cái bù shuō ， xiàn zài cái shuō ？ - Why are you just telling me now?

Lesson from HSK 4, Ch 1

Additional Usage Rules

  1. 刚 can be used with with a temporal word, both before and after, while 刚才 cannot. Examples:
    1. 他刚来一会儿。
    2. 我上个月刚搬家。
  2. 刚 is an adverb, while 刚才 is a temporal noun, so it can be placed before a subject, whereas 刚 cannot:
    1. 刚才我去洗手间了。(correct)
    2. 刚我去洗手间了。(incorrect)
    3. 我刚/刚才去洗手间 (both correct)
