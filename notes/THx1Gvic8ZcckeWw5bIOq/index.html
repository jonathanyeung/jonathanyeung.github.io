月 yuè radical

月 yuè radical means moon, but it also meat! (肉)

  • 腿 tuǐ - leg
  • 脚 jiǎo - foot
  • 胖 pàng - fat
  • 肺 fèi - lung
  • 胳膊 gēbó - arm
  • 脖子 bózi - neck

Usually, when written on the left side it means 'meat' and 'time' when written on the right side
