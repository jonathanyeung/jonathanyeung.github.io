## Class Notes

瞧不起 qiáo bù qǐ - look down on

高分低能 gāo fēn dī néng - High score low energy; someone who has a lot of talent but doesn't put it to good use.

**次** - secondary meaning of 次 is 'inferior'

- 他很次 tā hěn cì - He was inferior
