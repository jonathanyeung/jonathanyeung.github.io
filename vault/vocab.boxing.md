### Punches

- Punch - 拳 quán
- Jab / Cross 直拳 zhí quán 
- Uppercut - 勾拳 gōu quán
- Hook - 摆拳 bǎi quán （bǎi means swing)


### Movement

- 往左 wǎng zuǒ - Go to the left
- 往右 wǎng yòu - To the right

- 往前 wǎng qián - Forward
- 往后 wǎng hòu - Backward
